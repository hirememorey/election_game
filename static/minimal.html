Game Help

Objective

Win the Presidency by gaining the most influence through strategic actions.

Actions

  • Fundraise: Gain 5 PC (plus bonuses)
  • Network: Gain 2 PC + 1 Political Favor
  • Sponsor Legislation: Propose a bill (costs PC)
  • Declare Candidacy: Run for office (Round 4 only)
  • Use Favor: Use a political favor for special effects
  • Support/Oppose Legislation: Commit PC to legislation votes

Game Flow

Each round you get 2 Action Points (AP). Use them wisely to build your resources and influence.
